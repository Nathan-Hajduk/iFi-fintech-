Banking & Connections

iFi connects with over 12,000 financial institutions across the United States, including major banks, credit unions, investment platforms, and crypto exchanges. We use secure API connections through Plaid to safely link your accounts without storing your banking credentials.

iFi automatically syncs your transactions multiple times per day to ensure your financial data is always up-to-date. Most transactions appear within a few hours of posting to your bank account. You can also manually refresh your accounts at any time from the dashboard.

While we support most financial institutions, you can manually add accounts that aren't available for automatic connection. You can also request support for specific institutions by contacting our team, and we'll work to add them to our network.

Technical Support
