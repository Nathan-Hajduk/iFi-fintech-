Account & Security

Creating an iFi account is simple and takes just a few minutes. Click the "Sign Up" button in the header, provide your email address, create a secure password, and verify your email. You can then connect your bank accounts and start managing your finances immediately.

Absolutely. iFi uses bank-level 256-bit encryption to protect your data. We employ multi-factor authentication, and your financial information is stored using the same security standards as major financial institutions. We never sell your data to third parties, and we're SOC 2 Type II certified.

Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page. Enter your registered email address, and we'll send you a secure link to reset your password. The link expires after 1 hour for security purposes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact support.

Features & Functionality

iFi's AI analyzes your spending patterns, income, and financial goals to create personalized budgets. It learns from your behavior, identifies trends, and suggests optimizations. The AI considers factors like seasonal expenses, inflation rates, and your risk tolerance to provide actionable recommendations that adapt as your life changes.

Banking & Connections

iFi connects with over 12,000 financial institutions across the United States, including major banks, credit unions, investment platforms, and crypto exchanges. We use secure API connections through Plaid to safely link your accounts without storing your banking credentials.

iFi automatically syncs your transactions multiple times per day to ensure your financial data is always up-to-date. Most transactions appear within a few hours of posting to your bank account. You can also manually refresh your accounts at any time from the dashboard.

While we support most financial institutions, you can manually add accounts that aren't available for automatic connection. You can also request support for specific institutions by contacting our team, and we'll work to add them to our network.
