Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 5, 2026

Effective Date: January 5, 2026

Your Privacy Matters: iFi is committed to protecting your personal and financial information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data in compliance with applicable laws including CFPB regulations, FTC guidelines, and state privacy laws.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we collect:

  • Identity Information: Name, email address, username
  • Contact Information: Email address for communications
  • Account Credentials: Password (stored as encrypted hash)
  • Profile Information: Optional profile details you provide

1.2 Financial Information

Financial data you voluntarily input into iFi:

  • Income sources and amounts
  • Expense categories and amounts
  • Budget allocations
  • Debt balances and terms
  • Savings goals and progress
  • Investment holdings (self-reported, not connected to brokerage accounts)
  • Net worth calculations

Important: iFi does NOT connect to bank accounts or brokerage accounts. All financial data is manually entered by you.

1.3 Usage Data

  • Pages visited and features used
  • Session duration and frequency
  • Device information (browser, OS, screen resolution)
  • IP address and general location (city/state level)
  • AI query history and interactions

1.4 Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Authentication and session management
  • Security and fraud prevention
  • Preference storage (theme, language)

We do NOT use third-party advertising cookies or sell your data to advertisers.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Service Provision

  • Display your financial data and visualizations
  • Generate personalized AI insights and recommendations
  • Calculate financial metrics (net worth, debt ratios, etc.)
  • Track progress toward your financial goals
  • Provide budgeting and planning tools

2.2 Service Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns to improve features
  • Train and improve AI models (using anonymized data)
  • Fix bugs and enhance performance
  • Conduct security audits

2.3 Communication

  • Send account-related notifications
  • Respond to support requests
  • Notify about Terms or Privacy Policy changes
  • Send optional newsletters (with your consent)

3. Data Security Measures

3.1 Encryption

  • In Transit: TLS 1.3 encryption for all data transmission
  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption for database storage
  • Passwords: Bcrypt hashing with salt (never stored in plain text)

3.2 Access Controls

  • Role-based access control (RBAC) for internal systems
  • Multi-factor authentication for administrative access
  • Regular access audits and review
  • Principle of least privilege

3.3 Infrastructure Security

  • Secure cloud hosting with reputable providers
  • Regular security patches and updates
  • Firewall and intrusion detection systems
  • DDoS protection
  • Automated backups (encrypted)

3.4 Security Monitoring

  • 24/7 automated threat detection
  • Regular penetration testing
  • Vulnerability scanning
  • Incident response procedures

4. Data Retention Policy

Data Type Retention Period Reason
Account Information Duration of account + 90 days Account management, legal compliance
Financial Data Duration of account + 30 days Service provision, user convenience
AI Interaction Logs 12 months Model improvement, quality assurance
Usage Analytics 24 months (anonymized) Service improvement, trend analysis
Security Logs 7 years Legal compliance, fraud prevention
Backup Data 90 days (rolling) Disaster recovery

Account Deletion: When you delete your account, all personal financial data is permanently deleted within 30 days. Anonymized analytics data may be retained for service improvement.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

iFi does NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal or financial information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 Service Providers

We share limited data with trusted service providers who assist in operations:

  • Cloud Hosting: Secure servers for data storage
  • AI Services: OpenAI API for generating insights (data not used for training their models per our agreement)
  • Analytics: Anonymized usage data for service improvement
  • Email Service: Transactional email delivery

All service providers sign Data Processing Agreements and are bound by confidentiality obligations.

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law:

  • In response to valid subpoenas or court orders
  • To comply with regulatory investigations (SEC, FINRA, CFPB, FTC)
  • To protect rights, safety, or property of iFi or users
  • To prevent fraud or illegal activity

5.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred. Users will be notified and given the option to delete their account before the transfer.

6. Your Privacy Rights

6.1 Access and Portability

  • View Your Data: Access all personal data we store via Settings page
  • Export Your Data: Download your financial data in JSON/CSV format
  • Request Copy: Email legal@ifi-finance.com for a complete data copy

6.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update personal information anytime in Settings
  • Correct financial data entries
  • Request corrections via legal@ifi-finance.com

6.3 Deletion Rights

  • Account Deletion: Permanently delete your account and all associated data
  • Selective Deletion: Remove specific financial entries
  • Right to be Forgotten: Request complete data erasure (subject to legal retention requirements)

6.4 Opt-Out Rights

  • Opt out of marketing emails (unsubscribe link in emails)
  • Disable optional analytics tracking
  • Restrict AI insight generation

6.5 State-Specific Rights

California (CCPA/CPRA): Right to know, delete, opt-out of sale (we don't sell data), and non-discrimination.

Virginia, Colorado, Connecticut: Similar rights as CCPA including access, correction, deletion, and opt-out.

To exercise these rights, contact: privacy@ifi-finance.com or use the Settings page.

7. Children's Privacy

iFi is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18. If we discover such data has been collected, we will delete it immediately. Parents or guardians who believe their child has provided information should contact us at privacy@ifi-finance.com.

8. International Data Transfers

iFi operates primarily in the United States. If you access the Service from outside the US, your data may be transferred to and processed in the US, which may have different data protection laws. By using iFi, you consent to such transfers.

9. Third-Party Links

iFi may contain links to external websites (educational resources, financial institutions). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Material changes will be communicated via:

  • Email notification to registered users
  • Prominent notice on the website
  • In-app notification upon next login

Changes become effective 30 days after notification. Continued use after that date constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact Information

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@ifi-finance.com
  • Data Protection Officer: dpo@ifi-finance.com
  • Contact Form: Contact Us
  • Mail: iFi Financial Technologies, Privacy Team, 123 Finance St, Suite 400, Wilmington, DE 19801

Response Time: We will respond to privacy requests within 30 days (45 days for complex requests).

12. Regulatory Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with:

  • CFPB: Consumer Financial Protection Bureau consumer data guidelines
  • FTC: Federal Trade Commission privacy requirements
  • CCPA/CPRA: California Consumer Privacy Act and amendments
  • State Privacy Laws: Virginia CDPA, Colorado CPA, Connecticut CTDPA
  • GLBA: Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (where applicable)
