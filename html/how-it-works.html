Step 3: Access Powerful Financial Tools

Each page is built with specific functionality to manage different aspects of your finances.

Transactions Page

What You Can Do:

  • Add Transaction Modal - Radio buttons for income/expense type
  • Summary Cards - Total Income, Total Expenses, Net Cash Flow (updated per month)
  • Filters & Search - By category (food, transportation, utilities, etc.), type, and period
  • Transaction Grid - Dynamically loaded with id="transactionsGrid"
Add Transaction Form

Financial Goals

What You Can Do:

  • Goal Cards - Each goal shows target date, progress bar, and status (on-track/behind)
  • Progress Tracking - Visual progress with percentage: style="width: 52.5%"
  • Goal Details - Monthly contribution, remaining amount, months/years left
  • Actions - Adjust goals or add funds directly

Debt Manager

What You Can Do:

  • Total Debt Display - Sum of all outstanding balances
  • Interest Burn Rate - Shows monthly and yearly interest costs
  • Debt-Free Date - Calculated based on current payment strategy
  • Debt List - Each debt shows APR, monthly payment, payoff date, and progress bar
  • Strategy Comparison - Current, Avalanche (Highest APR First), Snowball (Smallest Balance First)
