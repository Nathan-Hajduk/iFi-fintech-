Step 3: Access Powerful Financial Tools

Each page is built with specific functionality to manage different aspects of your finances.

Transactions Page

What You Can Do:

  • Add Transactions - Quick modal to log income or expenses with categories
  • Summary Cards - Total Income, Total Expenses, Net Cash Flow updated per month
  • Smart Filters - Filter by category (food, transportation, utilities), type, and period
  • Transaction Grid - View all your transactions in an organized, searchable list
  • Auto-Categorization - iFi AI automatically categorizes your spending
  • Subscription Tracking - Never miss recurring payments with alerts

Financial Goals

What You Can Do:

  • Goal Cards - Visual cards showing target date, progress bar, and on-track status
  • Progress Tracking - Real-time progress bars with percentage completion
  • Goal Details - Monthly contribution, remaining amount, and timeline
  • Flexible Actions - Adjust goals or add funds directly from the page
  • Multiple Goals - Track home down payment, emergency fund, vacation, and more
  • AI Recommendations - Get suggestions on optimal contribution amounts

Debt Manager

What You Can Do:

  • Total Debt Display - See sum of all outstanding balances at a glance
  • Interest Burn Rate - Track monthly and yearly interest costs
  • Debt-Free Date - Calculated based on your current payment strategy
  • Detailed Debt List - Each debt shows APR, monthly payment, payoff date, and progress
  • Strategy Comparison - Compare Avalanche vs Snowball payoff methods
  • Savings Calculator - See how much faster you can pay off debt and save on interest

Investment Portfolio

What You Can Do:

  • Portfolio Summary - Total value, today's change, and total gain/loss
  • Asset Allocation Chart - Visualize your portfolio by type or by sector
  • Performance Chart - Track performance across multiple timeframes (1D, 1W, 1M, 3M, 1Y, ALL)
  • Holdings Table - Detailed view of all holdings with prices, shares, and gains
  • Smart Filters - Filter by asset type (stocks, ETFs, crypto, bonds, real estate)
  • Real-Time Prices - Live market data for all your investments

Today's Economy

What You Can Do:

  • Real-Time Market Indices - Track S&P 500, Dow Jones, and Nasdaq with live prices
  • Major Stock Cards - Monitor AAPL, MSFT, GOOGL, AMZN, TSLA and more
  • Auto-Refresh - Market data updates every 5 seconds automatically
  • Color-Coded Changes - Green for gains, red for losses at a glance
  • High/Low Tracking - See daily high and low prices for each asset
  • Personalized Feed - iFi AI curates news relevant to your investments
